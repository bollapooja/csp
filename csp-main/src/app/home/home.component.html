Chilli Crop

Chilli is used as a principal ingredient in various curries, also used in vegetables, spices, condiments, and pickles. Pungency in chillies is due to the active constituent "Capsaicin," an alkaloid.

Cotton Crop

Rotate your cotton crop as needed with other field or vegetable crops to maintain soil productivity and reduce the incidence of various cotton pests.

Tomato Crop

Tomatoes rive in soil rich in minerals and micronutrientsincluding phosphorus, calcium and magnesium. Ph should be of 5.8 to 7.0.

Corn Crop

Beside soyabeans, we can rotate maize with cowpeas; brassicas such as kales, cabbages, cauliflower, solanaceae crops such as potatoes and eggplant.

Rice Crop

In paddy-upland rotation system, paddy rice is generally cultivated int the summer season (late june to October) followed by upland crops in the winter.

Sugarcane Crop

I heavy rainfall areas sugar cane is rotated with rice. In low rainfall areas sugarcane is rotated with cotton.
